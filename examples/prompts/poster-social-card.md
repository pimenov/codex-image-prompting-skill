# Example: poster / social card

User request:

```text
Prepare a prompt for a square social card promoting a workshop about practical AI workflows.
```

Polished prompt:

```text
Square 1:1 social card for a professional workshop about practical AI workflows.

Promotional hierarchy:
1. Main title largest: "Practical AI workflows"
2. Supporting line: "From messy requests to repeatable systems"
3. CTA: "Workshop series"

Composition:
A calm operator stands beside a clean workflow board. The board shows three large abstract zones: intake, context, and decision. A thin glowing line connects notes, documents, and small agent terminal icons. Leave clean negative space around the title.

Style:
Editorial technology poster, restrained palette, warm off-white background, graphite text, muted blue accents, subtle paper texture, precise geometric composition.

Quality constraints:
Crisp typography, clear three-level hierarchy, readable at thumbnail size, no fake logos, no random extra text, no cluttered microtext.
```
